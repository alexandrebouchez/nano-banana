# Common Image Editing Patterns

## Background Changes
- "Change the background to solid dark blue"
- "Replace the background with a sunset scene"
- "Make the background blurred (bokeh effect)"

## Color Adjustments
- "Shift the color palette to warmer tones"
- "Make the overall image more vibrant"
- "Convert to monochrome / grayscale"

## Adding Elements
- "Add a subtle shadow below the object"
- "Add text 'HELLO' in the top right corner"
- "Add a decorative border"

## Removing Elements
- "Remove the text from the image"
- "Remove the background objects"
- "Clean up the edges"

## Style Changes
- "Make it look more like a watercolor painting"
- "Apply a minimalist flat design style"
- "Make it look more photorealistic"

## Refinement
- "Increase the contrast"
- "Make the details sharper"
- "Soften the lighting"

## Tips
- Be specific about what to change and what to preserve
- The model preserves the overall composition during edits
- For major changes, regenerating may be better than editing
- Use `--ref` to guide the edit with a style reference
